## QUICK REFERENCE: MINNESOTA COURT TIMELINES

**Conciliation Court (Small Claims):**
- Defendant has 20 days to respond after service
- Court date typically scheduled 4-8 weeks after filing
- No right to appeal for claims under $4,000

**District Court (Civil):**
- Answer due 21 days after service (30 days if served by publication)
- Discovery period varies by case
- Trial scheduled by court based on availability
