## ‚öñÔ∏è LEGAL THEORIES & CLAIMS

### Potential Claims Against Concepts 26
1. **Breach of Contract**
   - Failure to perform according to specifications
   - Missed deadlines
   - Substandard work quality

2. **Breach of Express Warranties**
   - Failed to meet promised capabilities
   - Work not fit for particular purpose

3. **Breach of Implied Warranty of Workmanlike Performance**
   - Work not performed to industry standards
   - Defective or incomplete performance

4. **Fraudulent Misrepresentation**
   - False statements about capabilities
   - Concealment of material facts
   - Reliance and damages

### Damages
- **Direct Damages:** Cost to complete work properly
- **Consequential Damages:** Lost profits, business opportunities
- **Incidental Damages:** Additional costs incurred
- **Cover Damages:** Difference in cost for replacement services

### Concepts 26's Likely Defenses
- Client breach/failure to cooperate
- Scope creep beyond original contract
- Force majeure/unforeseeable circumstances
- Work performed satisfactorily
- Damages not proximately caused
