# PETITION BUNDLE ASSEMBLY CHECKLIST
## Mississippi Chancery Court Filing Package

---

## OVERVIEW

This checklist guides you through assembling a complete petition bundle for filing with the Mississippi Chancery Court to request release of your original birth certificate.

---

## REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

### Core Documents (REQUIRED)

- [ ] **Cover Sheet** - Completed Chancery Clerk Cover Sheet
  - File: `Greta_BC_Petition_CoverSheet.md` (or fillable PDF)
  - Status: [ ] Template [ ] Completed [ ] Printed
  - All fields filled in accurately
  - County and district information verified

- [ ] **Petition** - Petition for Release of Original Birth Certificate
  - File: `Greta_BC_Petition_Release.md`
  - Status: [ ] Template [ ] Completed [ ] Printed [ ] Signed
  - All bracketed fields completed
  - Personal information accurate
  - Date and signature included

- [ ] **Affidavit** - Affidavit in Support of Petition
  - File: `Greta_BC_Affidavit.md`
  - Status: [ ] Template [ ] Completed [ ] Notarized
  - All statements reviewed for accuracy
  - Signed before notary public
  - Notary seal and signature present

---

## SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS (RECOMMENDED)

### DNA and Genealogical Evidence

- [ ] **DNA Match Analysis**
  - Summary of DNA testing results
  - Chart showing biological relative matches
  - Explanation of triangulated clusters
  - Contact information for DNA testing service used

- [ ] **Genealogical Research Documentation**
  - Family tree or pedigree chart
  - Research notes and findings
  - Timeline of research efforts
  - List of resources consulted

- [ ] **Outreach Correspondence**
  - Letters or emails to/from biological relatives
  - Responses from DNA matches
  - Documentation of attempts to confirm identity
  - Redact sensitive information as appropriate

### Medical Documentation

- [ ] **Medical Necessity Letter** (if applicable)
  - Letter from physician explaining need for family medical history
  - Documentation of health conditions requiring genetic information
  - Statement about importance of biological family medical records

- [ ] **Personal Health Statement**
  - Summary of health concerns
  - Questions about genetic conditions
  - Reasons why biological family medical history is needed

### Additional Support

- [ ] **Personal Statement**
  - Letter explaining your situation and request
  - Impact of not knowing your identity
  - Emotional and psychological effects
  - Good faith intentions

- [ ] **Previous Correspondence with Bureau of Vital Records**
  - Letters requesting information
  - Responses received
  - Documentation of attempts to access records through normal channels

## DOCUMENT PREPARATION CHECKLIST

### Before Printing

- [ ] All templates converted to final format (Word .docx or PDF)
- [ ] All bracketed fields [like this] completed
- [ ] Dates added to all documents
- [ ] Contact information verified and current
- [ ] Spelling and grammar checked
- [ ] Legal citations verified (Miss. Code Ann. § 93-17-21)

### Printing and Assembly

- [ ] All documents printed on white paper
- [ ] Black ink used for all signatures
- [ ] Pages numbered (if required by court)
- [ ] Documents in proper order (see assembly order below)
- [ ] Holes punched consistently (if required)
- [ ] Documents stapled or bound as required by court rules

### Signatures and Notarization

- [ ] Petition signed and dated by you
- [ ] Affidavit signed before notary public (DO NOT pre-sign)
- [ ] Notary acknowledgment completed
- [ ] Notary seal affixed
- [ ] All signature dates are current
