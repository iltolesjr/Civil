## ‚ùì QUICK FAQ

**Q: Can I file this myself?**  
A: Yes! You can file "pro se" (without a lawyer).

**Q: Will the court definitely approve this?**  
A: Not guaranteed, but adult adoptees increasingly win these cases, especially with DNA evidence.

**Q: What if I don't know the adoption county?**  
A: Start with Hinds County (where you were born). See Venue_Routing_Instructions.md.

**Q: Do I need to attend a hearing?**  
A: Maybe. The court will notify you if a hearing is required.

**Q: What if I can't afford the filing fee?**  
A: Include an Affidavit of Inability to Pay with your petition.
