# VENUE ROUTING INSTRUCTIONS
## Mississippi Chancery Court Filing Location

---

## DETERMINING PROPER VENUE

The correct venue for filing this petition is determined by the location where the adoption was finalized.

---

## STEP 1: IDENTIFY COUNTY OF ADOPTION

**Methods to Determine County:**

1. **Review Adoption Records** (if available)
   - Check any adoption paperwork you have
   - Look for court documents or decrees
   - Note the county listed on adoption documents

2. **Contact Mississippi Bureau of Vital Records**
   - Request information about where your adoption was processed
   - Phone: (601) 576-7981
   - Address: Mississippi State Department of Health, P.O. Box 1700, Jackson, MS 39215-1700

3. **Use DNA and Genealogical Research**
   - Review locations of biological relatives identified through DNA
   - Consider where your biological mother was likely living in 1971
   - Check adoption agency records if known

4. **Check Your Birth Location**
   - You were born in Jackson, Mississippi on December 23, 1971
   - Jackson is in Hinds County
   - If adoption occurred shortly after birth, it may have been in Hinds County

---

## STEP 2: DETERMINE CHANCERY COURT DISTRICT

**Resource:** [Mississippi Chancery Court District Map](https://courts.ms.gov/trialcourts/chancerycourt/chancerycourt.php)

### Mississippi Chancery Court Districts

Mississippi has **20 Chancery Court Districts**, each covering one or more counties.

**Common Districts for Central Mississippi:**

- **First District:** Hinds County (Jackson area)
- **Seventh District:** Includes several central Mississippi counties
- **Ninth District:** Madison and Rankin Counties (near Jackson)

### How to Confirm Your District:

1. Visit the Mississippi Courts website: https://courts.ms.gov/trialcourts/chancerycourt/chancerycourt.php

2. Click on the "Chancery Court District Map" link

3. Locate your county on the map

4. Note the district number and chancellor name

---

## STEP 3: LOCATE THE CHANCERY CLERK OFFICE

Once you've identified the correct county and district:

1. **Find the Chancery Clerk's Office**
   - Search online for "[County Name] Chancery Clerk Mississippi"
   - Call the clerk's office to confirm filing procedures

2. **Get Contact Information**
   - Physical address for in-person filing
   - Mailing address for mail filing
   - Phone number for questions
   - Office hours

3. **Inquire About E-Filing**
   - Ask if electronic filing is available
   - Request e-filing instructions if applicable

## FILING OPTIONS

### Option 1: In-Person Filing
- Take completed petition bundle to Chancery Clerk's office
- Pay filing fee or submit fee waiver request
- Obtain file-stamped copies for your records

### Option 2: Mail Filing
- Mail completed petition bundle with filing fee to Chancery Clerk
- Include self-addressed stamped envelope for file-stamped copies
- Use certified mail with return receipt for proof of filing

### Option 3: Electronic Filing (if available)
- Check with Chancery Clerk if e-filing is available in your district
- Follow district-specific e-filing procedures
- Retain electronic confirmation of filing

---

## FILING FEES

**Standard Chancery Court Filing Fees** (as of 2025):
- Filing fee typically ranges from $100-$200
- Exact fee varies by county
- Call Chancery Clerk's office to confirm current fee

**Fee Waiver Option:**
- If unable to pay filing fee, submit an "Affidavit of Inability to Pay"
- Provide documentation of financial hardship
- Court will review and may waive fees
