## STEP-BY-STEP: CHECKING ONLINE

### Minnesota Court Public Access (MPA)

1. **Go to the website:**
   - Visit: https://pa.courts.state.mn.us/CaseSearch

2. **Select Search Type:**
   - Choose "Case Number" if you have it
   - OR choose "Party Name" to search by your name

3. **Enter Information:**
   - **For Case Number:** Enter 62-CO-25-3554
   - **For Party Name:** Enter "Ira Toles" or "Toles, Ira"

4. **Select County:**
   - Ramsey County (for case 62-CO-25-3554)
   - Or select "All Counties" if unsure

5. **View Results:**
   - Click on your case number
   - Look for:
     - "Next Event Date"
     - "Hearing Date"
     - "Trial Date"
     - "Events and Hearings" section

6. **Check Case Events:**
   - Scroll to "Events and Hearings"
   - Look for scheduled dates
   - Note the time, location, and type of hearing

## IMPORTANT TIMELINES

### After Filing (Conciliation Court):
- **Day 0:** Case filed (July 17, 2025 for case 62-CO-25-3554)
- **Days 1-20:** Defendant must be served
- **Days 21-40:** Defendant has 20 days to respond after service
- **Weeks 4-8:** Hearing typically scheduled

### Typical Court Date Notice:
- You should receive notice **at least 14 days** before hearing
- Notice may come by mail or be available online
- Check online weekly if you haven't received notice

---

## IF YOU CANNOT FIND YOUR COURT DATE

### Possible Reasons:
1. **Not Yet Scheduled:** Court is still processing
2. **Waiting for Service:** Defendant hasn't been served yet
3. **Waiting for Response:** Court waiting for defendant to respond
4. **Administrative Delay:** Court backlog

### What to Do:
1. **Wait 4-6 weeks** after filing
2. **Check online weekly**
3. **Call court clerk** if no date after 6 weeks
4. **Keep copies** of all filed documents
