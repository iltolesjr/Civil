# Jeff Mills Case — Working Folder

This folder organizes pleadings, evidence, correspondence, research, and filing records so you can assemble a complete packet quickly and stay ready if lawyers get involved.

Quick map:
- 01_Pleadings: Core documents filed with the court (statement of claims, affidavit, complaint drafts).
- 02_Evidence: Photos, exhibits index, and chain of custody.
- 03_Correspondence: Email log and conversation transcripts.
- 04_Chronology: Master timeline mapping events to evidence and legal significance.
- 05_Legal_Research: Statutes and case-law notes.
- 06_Demand_and_Negotiation: Demand letter and settlement notes.
- 07_Strategy: Issues matrix, witness list, questions, hearing prep checklist.
- 08_Filings_and_Service: Service log and filing receipts.
- 09_Drafts_and_Working: Scratch area, revision notes.
- 10_Admin_Agency: Plans for agency filings (AG, housing authority, HUD, etc.).

Helpful references:
- See `00_Glossary.md` for plain-language legal terms.
- See `00_Filing_Options.md` for where else to file complaints.

How to use:
1) Keep Statement of Claims as your single source of truth for parties, amounts, and facts.
2) When adding evidence, update `02_Evidence/exhibits_index.csv` and link it in timeline and pleadings.
3) Cross-reference everything: timeline ←→ issues matrix ←→ exhibits.
4) Before hearings, complete `07_Strategy/hearing_prep_checklist.md` and print exhibits list.
